About Uptown Catering

Uptown Catering is dedicated to providing the best catering experiences in Greenville and upstate South Carolina and the surrounding areas. Our events are tailored to your tastes and styles. We provide food and decor fitting for many great local events and venues. Uptown Catering takes great pleasure in providing you with meals showing the utmost of care.

Your food needs are our greatest concern, no matter the size of your group. We provide services for a broad range of events from small luncheons to exquisite dinners for over 500 guests. Whatever your need is, we consider it our mission to provide the best service available.

If you have the perfect idea in mind, we're eager to make it a reality. But we offer creative suggestions and solutions based on you and your tastes to make an event fitting to your occasion.
