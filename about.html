About Uptown Catering

Uptown Catering is dedicated to providing the best catering experiences in Greenville, the South Carolina Upstate, and surrounding areas. Our events are tailored to your tastes and styles. We provide food and decor suited to local events and venues. Uptown Catering takes great pleasure in providing you with food and service exhibiting the utmost of care.

Your food needs are our greatest concern, no matter the size of your group. We provide services for a broad range of events from small luncheons to exquisite dinners for over 500 guests. Whatever your need is, we consider it our mission to provide the best service available.

In order to cater to you, at Uptown Catering we listen. We understand that your event is unique, and we want to ensure that you get exactly what you are looking for. If you have the perfect idea in mind, we're eager to make it a reality. We also offer creative suggestions and solutions best fit to you and your tastes to make an event perfect for your occasion.

Whether your event is in 18 months or in 18 hours, call us today. We look forward to working with you.
